# Decisions

Append-only record of non-trivial decisions. Newest at the top.

Each entry captures the call, the reasoning, the alternatives that lost, who owns the outcome, and the trigger that would make {{OWNER_NAME}} revisit. Scanning this file should make six-months-from-now-{{OWNER_NAME}} confident about (or surprised by) past thinking — not lost.

The assistant maintains this file via the `decision-log` skill. To log a decision in conversation, say "log a decision" or just "we're going with X" and the assistant will draft the entry and append it here.
